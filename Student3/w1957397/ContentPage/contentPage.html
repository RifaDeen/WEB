Meet the Best of the Best

Top 4 Current Volleyball Players in the World-Men



Ivan Zaytsev

Full name: Ivan Vyacheslavovich

Nationality: Italian

Achievements:

  • Captain of Italy's men's national volleyball team
  • Bronze medalist at the 2012 Olympic Games in London
  • Silver medalist at both the 2011 and 2013 European Championships
  • Bronze medalist at the 2013 and 2014 World League competitions
  • Italian Championship, and silver medalist at the 2016 Olympic Games


Giba

Full name: Gilberto Amauri Filho

Nationality: Brazilian

Achievements:

  • FIVB U19 World Championship
  • FIVB U21 World Championship
  • Gold medalist at FIVB world League
  • Gold medalist at Olympic games in 2011
  • FIVB U21 World Championship Most Valuable Player
  • 2011 Sky Sports The most famous Volleyball player in the World


Earvin Gapeth

Full name: Earvin N'Gapeth

Nationality: French

Achievements:

  • 2015 European Champion
  • Gold medallist of the World League (2015 and 2017)
  • French Champion (2010)
  • Italian Champion (2016)
  • Olympic Champion (2020)
  • 2011 Sky Sports The most famous Volleyball player in the World


Maxim Mikhaylov

Full name: Maxim Mikhaylov

Nationality: Russian

Achievements:

  • Gold medalist of FIVB U21 World Championship
  • Gold medallist of the World League (2015 and 2017)
  • Gold medallist of FIVB Nations League(2010)
  • FIVB World Cup (2011)
  • FIVB World League(2013)
  • 2017 CEV Champions League-Most Valuable Player

All-Time Famous Volleyball legends



Karch Kiraly

Nationality: American

Years Served: 1980-1992

Achievements:

  • FIVB Best Player in the World (1986, 1988)
  • FIVB Best Player of the 20th Century
  • NCAA Volleyball Tournament Most Outstanding Player (1981, 1982)
  • UCLA Hall of Fame (inducted 1992)


Lorenzo Bernardi

Nationality: Italian

Years Served: 1990-2007

Achievements:

  • 1992: FIVB World League, Most Valuable Player
  • 1992: FIVB World Championship, Most Valuable Player
  • 1996: FIVB World League - Best Scorer
  • Knight of the Order of Merit


Saeid Marouf

Nationality: Iranian

Years Served: 2007-2017

Achievements:

  • World Grand Champions Cup-Bronze medal
  • European Champions League-Gold medal
  • Best Setter: 2008 3rd World Olympic Qualification Tournament
  • Best Asian Athlete of the Year: 2016


Sheilla Castro

Nationality: Brazilian

Years Served: 1997-2017

Achievements:

  • 2005 FIVB World Grand Champions Cup - "Most Valuable Player"
  • 2005-06 CEV Cup - "Best Spiker"
  • 2009-10 Brazilian Superliga - "Best Server"
  • 2016 FIVB World Grand Prix-"Best Outside Spiker"
